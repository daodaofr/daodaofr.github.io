📖 Biography

I am a tenure-track associate professor at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my Ph.D. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2019, supervised by Professor Xiaokang Yang. I received M.E. from École Centrale de Lyon in France, and B.E. from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2013. I was a research scientist at the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI) from June 2019 to April 2021.

My research interests include:

  • 🧊 3D Representation — neural rendering, 3D reconstruction, Gaussian splatting
  • 🎨 AIGC — generative models, digital humans, face/body modeling
  • 🤖 Embodied AI — humanoid teleoperation, human-robot interaction
